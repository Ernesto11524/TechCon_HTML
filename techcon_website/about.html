History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2012 as a small regional meetup for software developers and tech enthusiasts in Accra, Ghana. What started as an informal gathering of fewer than 100 attendees has grown into one of Africa’s most recognized and forward-thinking technology conferences. The goal from the beginning was simple: to create a space where innovation, collaboration, and learning could flourish.

Over the years, TechCon has evolved from a single-track developer event into a multi-day, multi-track international conference attracting thousands of participants from across the globe. Each year has marked a significant step forward—whether it was the introduction of the Women in Tech panel in 2015, the launch of startup showcases in 2018, or the pivot to hybrid conferencing in response to the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020.

The evolution of TechCon mirrors the explosive growth of the tech industry in Africa and beyond. By consistently embracing change and championing inclusion, TechCon has helped shape the careers of countless developers, entrepreneurs, and students. With each edition, it reaffirms its commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in technology.

As we look ahead to TechCon 2024, we also honor the legacy of past conferences that laid the foundation. From humble beginnings to a thriving tech movement, the history of TechCon is a testament to what’s possible when passionate people come together to build the future.

Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower current and future generations of tech leaders by creating a space where knowledge is shared, innovation is celebrated, and meaningful connections are made. We believe in technology as a force for good—capable of transforming lives, communities, and economies.

Our driving principles are grounded in inclusivity, collaboration, and sustainability. We are committed to making technology accessible to all, regardless of background or experience level. Whether you're a student, a startup founder, or a seasoned engineer, TechCon provides a platform where your voice matters.

Every session, workshop, and exhibition is designed with impact in mind. We aim not only to educate but to inspire action—encouraging participants to take what they learn and apply it in their own communities and industries. Through open dialogue, diverse perspectives, and cutting-edge ideas, TechCon fuels the engine of progress.

Building a future where everyone has the tools to shape technology and be shaped by it responsibly.
